# Quick Start Guide

Get playing in under 60 seconds!

## Instant Play (No Setup Required)

1. **Open the game**
   ```
   Simply double-click index.html
   ```
   Or drag it into your web browser.

2. **Wait for loading**
   - The game will load with placeholder graphics
   - Loading should take ~2 seconds

3. **Start playing!**
   - Player 1 (Left - Blue): Q, A, 1-3
   - Player 2 (Right - Red): E, D, 4-6

## Basic Strategy

### For Beginners
1. **Mana Management**: Each spell costs 40 mana, you can cast 2 spells before needing to regenerate
2. **Stamina Management**: Each swing costs 10 stamina, watch your green bar!
3. **Interrupt Enemy**: Hit them while they're casting to make their spell fizzle
4. **Time Your Attacks**: Wait for enemy to start casting, then swing!

### Advanced Techniques
1. **Explosion Combo**:
   - Cast Explosion (3s cast)
   - While explosion is traveling (2s), cast Energy Bolt
   - Both hit almost simultaneously = massive damage!

2. **Feinting**:
   - Start casting a spell
   - Press Q/E to cancel it and equip weapon
   - Enemy wastes their interrupt attempt
   - WARNING: You still lose the mana!

3. **Quick Lightning**:
   - Fastest spell (1.5s)
   - Use when enemy is low HP for quick finish
   - Or use to interrupt enemy casts

## Common Questions

**Q: Why did my spell fizzle?**
- You got hit while casting, OR
- You pressed Q/E to cancel it manually

**Q: Why can't I cast?**
- Not enough mana (need 40), OR
- Already casting another spell

**Q: Why can't I swing?**
- Weapon not equipped (press Q/E), OR
- Still on cooldown from last swing, OR
- Not enough stamina (need 10)

**Q: How do I win?**
- Reduce opponent's health to 0
- Watch the health bars above characters!

## Tips & Tricks

✅ **DO:**
- Watch enemy cast bar - interrupt when you see it!
- Manage your resources (mana/stamina regenerate when idle)
- Use explosion combo for maximum damage
- Swing weapon when you see enemy start casting

❌ **DON'T:**
- Spam spells without watching mana
- Forget to re-equip weapon after canceling
- Stand idle - you're an easy target!
- Use Lightning for everything - save it for killshots

## Victory Conditions

- First player to reach 0 HP loses
- Press "Restart Match" button to play again
- Or press R key

## Performance Issues?

If the game runs slowly:
1. Close other browser tabs
2. Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
3. Reduce browser zoom to 100%
4. Close other applications
